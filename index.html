About Our Village

Discover the rich heritage and natural splendor of Oorakam Kunnath

A Timeless Heritage

Nestled in the heart of Kerala, Oorakam Kunnath Village has stood as a testament to the rich cultural heritage of God's Own Country for centuries. Founded in the early 1800s, our village has preserved its traditional charm while embracing sustainable progress.

The village derives its name from "Oorakam" meaning "heart of the settlement" and "Kunnath" referring to the hillside location, perfectly capturing our geographical and cultural significance in the region.

Cultural Highlights

Experience the vibrant traditions that define our community

Kathakali & Folk Arts

Our village preserves ancient dance forms like Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, and Theyyam through regular performances and training programs for younger generations.

Temple Festivals

The annual Pooram festival at Sri Bhagavathy Temple attracts visitors from across Kerala with its procession of decorated elephants and traditional percussion ensembles.

Handloom Weaving

Oorakam Kunnath is renowned for its handloom industry, producing exquisite Kerala kasavu sarees and mundu using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

Traditional Cuisine

Our culinary heritage features authentic Kerala dishes like Karimeen Pollichathu, Appam with Stew, and the elaborate Sadya feast served during celebrations and festivals.

Eastern Village, 3.5km from center

Oorakam Spice Gardens

Take guided tours through aromatic spice plantations growing cardamom, pepper, vanilla, and other exotic spices.

Emerald Paddy Fields
